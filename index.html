Frequently Asked Questions for Global Buyers

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for international exports?
Moq varies by product; typically 1x20ft container for bulk commodities, but LCL (Less than Container Load) is available for premium spices like vanilla and cloves.
Does Naturra Extal provide customized packaging?
Yes, we offer custom bulk packaging ranging from 25kg multi-layer kraft paper bags for cocoa to customized retail packaging for private label partners.
How does Naturra Extal ensure product quality?
We implement a direct-from-farmer sourcing model with multiple inspection points, ensuring every shipment meets international safety and quality standards (including moisture content and purity tests).
